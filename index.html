Word Scramble Game

How to Play:

  1. A scrambled word will appear on the screen.
  2. You have 30 seconds to unscramble and guess the correct word.
  3. Type your answer in the input field provided.
  4. Click the "Check Word" button or press Enter to submit your guess.
  5. Points are awarded based on how quickly you answer:
    • 10 points for answers within the first 10 seconds
    • 8 points for answers between 10-15 seconds
    • 6 points for answers between 15-25 seconds
    • 4 points for answers in the last 5 seconds
  6. If your guess is incorrect, you'll be notified and can try again within the time limit.
  7. A hint will appear after 10 seconds if you're struggling with the word.
  8. If time runs out, the correct word will be revealed, and you'll move to the next word.
  9. There are 10 words to unscramble in each level (easy, medium, hard).
  10. After completing a level, you'll automatically move to the next difficulty level.
  11. Your score accumulates across all levels. The game ends after completing all three difficulty levels.
